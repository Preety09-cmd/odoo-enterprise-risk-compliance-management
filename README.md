# Enterprise Risk & Compliance Management for Odoo 17

## Overview

Enterprise Risk & Compliance Management is a custom Odoo 17 module designed to support organizational risk governance, assessment, treatment, monitoring, and reporting.

The solution enables organizations to identify risks, assign ownership, assess risk severity, manage mitigation activities, maintain evidence documentation, and track risk treatment decisions through a structured workflow.

## Key Features

- Risk Register Management
- Risk Assessment and Scoring
- Priority Classification
- Workflow Management
- Treatment Strategy Tracking
- Approval Workflow
- Review Activities
- Evidence Attachments
- PDF Reporting
- Risk Score Analysis
